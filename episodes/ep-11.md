## Takeaways

- The AI fluency pyramid helps assess AI integration levels.
- Open models are still dominated by Chinese companies.
- Claude Code is evolving with new swarm feature.
- The Claude Constitution aims to define ethical AI behavior.
- Economic disruption is a significant risk associated with AI.
