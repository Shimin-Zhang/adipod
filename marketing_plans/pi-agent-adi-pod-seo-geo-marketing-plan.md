## 4. GEO Strategy (Generative Engine Optimization)

### 4.1 What Is GEO and Why It Matters

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) ensures that when users ask AI assistants questions about topics the ADI Pod covers, the pod is **cited, recommended, or its insights surface** in the AI response.

**Why this matters now:**

- Perplexity has 15M+ daily queries, many from developers
- ChatGPT search is replacing Google for conversational queries
- Gemini is integrated into Google Search (AI Overviews)
- Claude's web search surfaces authoritative content
- Developers increasingly ask AI "what's a good podcast about X?" or "explain concept Y"

**The ADI Pod advantage:** With 194K words of timestamped, attributed, expert content — the pod has more citable, structured content than almost any competitor. The issue is making it *findable and parseable* by AI.

### 4.2 Structured Authority Signals

AI models prioritize content that demonstrates **E-E-A-T** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust). Here's how to signal each:

#### Experience
- Every blog post and pillar page should reference **hands-on testing** from Vibe & Tell segments
- Include first-person "we tested this" language: "In our testing across Episodes 13-15, we found that..."
- Quantify experiences: "After running 5 concurrent Claude Code agents for 3 weeks..."

#### Expertise
- Host bios on every page: "Shimin Zhang, software engineer and AI practitioner since 2023"
- Include specific data points from episodes: "According to CodeRabbit's study (covered in Ep 7), AI-authored PRs have 1.7x more findings..."
- Reference academic papers by name, DOI, and year

#### Authoritativeness
- Consistent bylines with author schema markup
- Cross-reference other authoritative sources discussed: Anthropic blog, ThoughtWorks, Oxford Internet Institute
- Use the format: "As [Name] from [Organization] explained in [Source], [claim]"

#### Trust
- Date every article prominently
- Update pillar pages visibly ("Last updated: March 2026")
- Include correction notices if needed
- Link to primary sources for all claims
